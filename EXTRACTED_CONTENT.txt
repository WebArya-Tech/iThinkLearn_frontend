The website will be developed in 4 phases. After each phase the website will be published. 
Phase 1: There will be only static information. Users will be able to reach us using the contact us tab where we will direct them to whatsapp. 
Phase 2: These dynamic pages will be added: blogs, ask a question, schedule a demo class, Get Free Counselling and currently running classes. OTP validation will be added too. The AI module will answer the question posted under ‘ask a question’. The fee payment module will be integrated too. ‘Feedbacks’ and ‘Reviews’ pages will be added too along with ‘create new’ forms.  
Phase 3: Sign up and sign in facility will be added. Students can sign in and see their info, lesson plan, homework, announcements and other updates. ‘Practice Test’ tab will be added too. Admin pages will be created to control these dynamic pages. The answer of the question posted under ‘ask a question’ will be reviewed by an online tutor and answered manually if needed. 
Phase 4: Instant tutor request will be added after otp validation. Students can instantly start a class. ‘Download study material’ page will be added.

Teaching Methodology
Teaching Methodology
At   iThinkLearn Tuitions, our teaching methodology is built on a simple belief:
 Strong concepts + consistent practice + personalized feedback = guaranteed academic success.
Over 25 years, we have developed a proven and structured learning framework that helps students understand deeply, retain confidence, and perform exceptionally in exams. Our approach eliminates fear, builds discipline, and makes learning meaningful and enjoyable.
Our Step-by-Step Learning Process
1. Diagnostic Assessment & Batch Allocation
We begin with an initial evaluation to understand each student’s current level, strengths, and areas that need improvement. Based on this, students are placed in 4–6 member batches with similar learning pace for maximum effectiveness.
2. Concept-Based Teaching
Every chapter is taught from the basics to advanced level, using real-life examples, diagrams, animations, and board-specific explanations.
 ✔ Focus on clarity, not memorization
 ✔ Emphasis on why, not just what
 ✔ Encouraging logical thinking, reasoning & application
3. Interactive Live Classes
Our sessions are engaging and discussion-based — not lecture-style one-way teaching.
 Students are encouraged to ask doubts, participate actively, and solve questions live.
4. Notes, Summaries & Learning Material
We provide:
Chapter-wise concise notes

Formula sheets and key concept summaries

Question banks, solved examples, and PYQs (Previous Year Questions)
 This makes revision faster and more effective.

5. Practice-Driven Learning
After completing each topic:
Worksheets & assignments are given

Students submit solutions for evaluation

Teachers provide corrections and improvement feedback
Practice is treated as a core learning element, not an optional activity.
6. Regular Tests & Performance Tracking
We conduct:
Weekly or chapter-wise tests

Monthly assessments

Pre-board and final revision tests

Each test is analyzed, and students receive detailed feedback.
Parents receive performance reports to track progress transparently.
7. Personalized Doubt-Solving Sessions
Special dedicated time is assigned for clearing doubts, revisiting weak topics, and one-on-one academic guidance.
8. Revision & Exam-Focused Preparation
As exams approach, we conduct:
Intensive revision classes

Solving of previous board questions

Time-bound mock tests for writing practice

Strategy sessions for exam-wise scoring

Our methodology ensures students enter the exam hall with confidence, clarity, and calmness.
